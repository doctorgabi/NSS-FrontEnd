  • What is the scheduled maintenance for my vehicle?
    • We encourage you to get oil changes every three thousand (3,000 mi) miles. For further detail, please refer to your manufacturer's established maintenance schedule--usually found in the car manual--or your trusted mechanic.
  • How can I obtain a copy of the CarFax or other information on my vehicle?
    • You may obtain a copy of the CarFax directly from their website
  • What are the benefits of the undercoat package?
    • The Ultra-Protection undercoat protects your car like a suit of armor protected knights going into battle. Rust is the enemy which over-time destroys all cars--the undercoat will extend the life of your vehicle immeasureably, making it a wise investment in your vehicle.
  • What's included in the twelve-point pre-owned professional inspection?
    • Our professional in-house mechanics perform the most thorough checks in the industry to ensure that you're always getting a quality, well-maintained new or pre-loved car, when you're doing business with us.
  • What warranties are offered on our luxury cars?
    • We offer our standard 100,000 or 10 year limited power-train warranty on all new vehicles. See our Car Consultants for details on warranties for our pre-loved vehicles or for more information on any of our warranties.
  • What is the Luxury ExperienceSM Difference?
    • The Luxury Experience starts from the moment you first contact us, and through all phases of your car ownership. We're here to make owning a car feel like a luxury experience from start to finish!
  • What options are available on our vehicles?
    • We offer all standard manufacturer packages on our vehicles, do some limited customizations in-house, and can refer you to trusted businesses in the area for any othe custom work or estimates.
  • Is that thing "street-legal"?
    • All our vehicles are "street-legal". While we know the joys of racetrack driving, and motorsport, we encourage everyone to obey the rules of the road.
  • How do I make a reservation?
    • Come by our campus or contact us
  • Other questions or comments?
    • Please feel free to contact us for additional information.
